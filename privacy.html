The company Swantzter located in Sweden operates RopeScore's web services. This page was last updated 2022-09-21.

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data being processed
  • Have your personal data erased
  • Have your personal data rectified
  • Request the processing to be restricted
  • Receive data in a commonly used format
  • Lodge a complaint with a Data Protection Authority

When using RopeScore and its online services your data may be transferred and/or processed by organisations outside of the European Union, when such processing occurs Swantzter has put appropriate safeguards in place to guarantee your rights and freedoms, primarily through standard contractual clauses.

Swantzter will will remove data once the purpose it was collected for is no longer relevant, such as when you unregister with us or cancel your business relationship with us.

For any inquiries about how Swantzter processes your data, please email privacy@swantzter.se

RopeScore core system

When using RopeScore, personal information such as names, club affiliations and sport nationality of participants and judges will be processed by Swantzter. This processing will happen in Google Cloud, region europe-west3 (Frankfurt). You can read more in Google Cloud's Contracts and Terms. This transfer outside the EU uses standard contractual clauses. This processing is based on the legal basis of our legitimate interest of providing you with the service.
